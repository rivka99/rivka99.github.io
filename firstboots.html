What is propaganda?

Propaganda is typically found in the form of news, advertisements or videos. It is often false or non complete information produced by governments or organizations in order to affect the opinions and beliefs of people. Propaganda can be found in many different forms, many times it can come from a seemingly reliable news source.

What is the "propaganda effect"?

The propaganda effect is a made up term, it is the idea of the negative social and political effects of propaganda. This page generally focuses on anti Israel propaganda and propaganda spread by Israel's enemies in the middle east.
